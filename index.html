Welcome to the website of LIV Livres Solidaires!

LIV is a small non-profit organization based in Belgium. We collect and sort leisure reading books from Belgium and the North of France, then ship them to Haiti. There, we receive them and prepare small libraries for education projects, schools and libraries. Along the distribution of books, we train headmasters and librarians to ensure books are well maintained and can be read by as many people as possible.

Our organization was created out of two main realizations. Firstly, there is an urgent need for reading materials in Haiti. While reading is universally recognized as a pillar of education, access to books is severely limited for the people of Haiti. Although elementary education is mandatory, many Haitian children have never read or held a book. Secondly, many people in Belgium struggle to find channels to donate their books. As a result, thousands upon thousands of books in good condition are recycled every year.

Please navigate this website to learn more about the actions we take to deal with these issues and how you can help us. Should you have any question, do not hesitate to send us an email at projetliv@gmail.com.

Since its creation in November 2012, LIV has shipped 6 containers, or more than 225,000 books, to Haiti.
